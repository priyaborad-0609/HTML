  • India's First Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV):

    Kalam was the project director for the SLV, which launched the Rohini satellite into orbit in 1980.

  • Pokhran-II nuclear tests:

    Kalam played a major role in these tests as a scientist.

  • Kalam National Memorial:

    The Defence Research and Development Organisation built this memorial in 2015 to honor Kalam.

  • APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University:

    The Kalam Foundation, which supports the vision of Kalam, has 15 state units and a few overseas units.

  • Books:

    Kalam wrote several books, including Wings of Fire, Turning Points: A Journey Through Challenges,
    My Journey: Transforming Dreams Into Actions,
    Reignited: Scientific Pathways to a Brighter Future, and
    The Luminous Sparks: A Biography in Verse and Colours.

  • Bharat Ratna:

    Kalam was awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.

Related

  • Beliefs

Dr. Kalam believed that making mistakes is a vital part of learning and growing. He also believed in the importance of ethics, integrity, and honesty.

  • Education

Kalam was a bright and hardworking student who studied physics and aeronautical engineering.

  • Career

- Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
- Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO)

  • Quotes

"Knowledge cannot be taken away from anyone except by obsolescence" and "If four things are followed - having a great aim, acquiring knowledge, hard work, and perseverance.
